Our Story

About SUG Chennai

We're a grassroots community of Sitecore enthusiasts passionate about building knowledge and connections in Chennai.

Our Mission

Sitecore User Group Chennai (SUG Chennai) is a group of like-minded professionals sharing their Sitecore experience with the community. Our objective is to promote the Sitecore ecosystem by hosting meetups and webinars at regular intervals.

This group is for everyone — not restricted to any specific geographic location or knowledge level, be it Novice to Expert on Sitecore. If you are passionate about digital experience, you belong here.
