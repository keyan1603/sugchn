Community Standards

Community Guidelines

Creating a respectful, inclusive environment for all Sitecore practitioners.

Be Respectful

Treat all community members with respect and kindness. We welcome professionals at all levels — from beginners to Sitecore MVPs. Condescending, dismissive, or harassing behavior of any kind is not tolerated.

Share Knowledge Freely

This community thrives on knowledge sharing. Share your experiences, code snippets, solutions, and learnings openly. Give credit where it's due.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to Sitecore, digital experience platforms, and related technology. Excessive off-topic promotion or spam will be moderated.

No Solicitation

This is not a recruiting or sales platform. Unsolicited job postings, sales pitches, or advertising are not permitted without prior approval from the organizers.

Confidentiality

Do not share confidential client information, internal company data, or proprietary code in community channels. Use anonymized examples where needed.

Reporting Issues

If you witness behavior that violates these guidelines, please contact the organizers. We will investigate and act accordingly.
